Enchilada Recipe

Following a good recipe is worth it for good enchiladas.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground Beef
  • Enchilada sauce
  • Cheese
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. First, preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Add a skillet over medium heat then add beef, season with salt and pepper. Stir in minced garlic. Add enchilada sauce.
  3. Lay out the tortillas and evenly add the beef mixture on top of them. Sprinkle some shredded cheese evenly on top the beef. Rollup the enchiladas.
  4. Using a dish, pur some enchilada sauce along the bottom of the dish
  5. Roll the enchiladas and lay on an oven safe pan. Pour remaining sauce on top. Cover with foil. Add to oven.
  6. Remove pan from oven. Let cool. And enjoy
